Requirements

Code Quality
Focuses on clean, maintainable, and well-structured code that adheres to industry standards and best practices.

Security Practices
Evaluates the application of secure coding principles, data protection, and proactive risk mitigation strategies.

Testing
Assesses the design and execution of tests (unit, integration, etc.) to ensure solution reliability and bug prevention.

Tool Dexterity
Looks at the proficiency and adaptability in utilizing relevant tools, frameworks, and technologies efficiently.

Solution Design
Examines the capability to create scalable, robust, and practical architectures that meet technical and business needs.

Cost Optimization
Reviews the effectiveness of the solution in managing resources efficiently and minimizing unnecessary operational costs.

DevOps Practices
Focuses on deployment strategies, use of version control, automation, CI/CD pipelines, and operational excellence.

Structure, Quality and Documentation
Evaluates the clarity of technical reporting, version control usage, and the comprehensiveness of documentation.

Problem Solving Approach
Assesses the ability to analyze complex problems, develop effective solutions, and articulate design decisions.
